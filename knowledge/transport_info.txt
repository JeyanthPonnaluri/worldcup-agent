[Wankhede Stadium Transit]
- Local Train: Churchgate Station (Western Line) is 200 meters away. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) is 2 km away.
- Bus: BEST bus routes to Marine Drive or Churchgate.
- Taxis: Prepaid taxi stand at Churchgate station.

[Narendra Modi Stadium Transit]
- Metro: Take Ahmedabad Metro Blue Line to Sabarmati Metro Station. Walk 500 meters from the station to the gate entrances.

[M Chinnaswamy Stadium Transit]
- Metro: Take Namma Metro Purple Line to Cubbon Park Metro Station, located directly opposite the stadium gate.
