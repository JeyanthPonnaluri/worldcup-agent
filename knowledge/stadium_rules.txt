[DSLR Camera Rules]
DSLR cameras, mirrorless cameras, professional cameras, video cameras, or recording equipment are strictly prohibited inside the stadium unless the fan possesses official media credentials. Standard smartphones are permitted for personal use.

[Prohibited Items]
Power banks are allowed but subject to security inspection. Backpacks, large bags (except clear bags under 12x6x12 inches), food, water bottles, cans, fireworks, banners with offensive language, and helmets are strictly prohibited inside the venue.

[Clear Bag Policy]
Only clear plastic bags not exceeding 12" x 6" x 12" are allowed. Clutch bags smaller than 4.5" x 6.5" are permitted after security screening.
