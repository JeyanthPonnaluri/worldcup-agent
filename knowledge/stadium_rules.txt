[Permitted Items & Smart Devices]
- Smartphones: Standard mobile phones and smartwatches are permitted for personal, non-commercial use.
- Power Banks: Personal power banks for mobile phone charging are permitted inside all 5 stadiums but must undergo physical security inspection.
- Medical Equipment: Essential medical kits, inhalers, and baby food/milk formulas are allowed subject to verification by security supervisors.
- Small Purses: Small clutches or wallets (not exceeding 4.5" x 6.5") are allowed after passing through metal detectors.

[Strictly Prohibited Items - All Venues]
- Recording Devices: DSLR cameras, mirrorless cameras, professional lenses, video cameras, GoPros, drones, selfie sticks, and tripods are strictly prohibited without official BCCI/ICC media accreditation.
- Dangerous Objects: Coins (which can be thrown), helmets, umbrellas (except small collapsible ones, but strictly banned in some stands), metal flasks, glass bottles, cans, lighters, matchboxes, firecrackers, vape devices, and e-cigarettes.
- Luggage & Bags: Backpacks, laptop bags, suitcase packages, and large handbags are strictly banned. There are NO locker facilities at the stadium gates.
- Offensive Materials: Flags, banners, or clothing containing political, offensive, commercial, or religious statements are not allowed.

[Clear Bag Policy]
Only transparent plastic, vinyl, or PVC clear bags not exceeding 12" x 6" x 12" are allowed into Wankhede Stadium, Narendra Modi Stadium, Chinnaswamy Stadium, Rajiv Gandhi Stadium, and Arun Jaitley Stadium. All non-clear bags will be turned away at security check-points.

[Indian Stadium Flags & Banners Policy]
Fans can bring national flags or team flags. However, flagpoles must be made of flexible plastic/cardboard and must not exceed 1 meter in length and 2 cm in diameter. Wooden or metal poles are strictly prohibited as potential weapons. Banners must not exceed 4 feet by 3 feet and must not obstruct the view of other spectators.
