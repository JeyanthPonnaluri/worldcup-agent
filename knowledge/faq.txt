Yes, personal power banks for mobile phone charging are allowed inside the stadium but will be inspected at the security checkpoint.

No, personal water bottles (including plastic and metal flasks) are not allowed inside. Complimentary drinking water is provided in cups throughout the concourse level.

[Gates Opening]
Stadium gates typically open 2 hours prior to kickoff. Fans are encouraged to arrive early to clear security checks.
