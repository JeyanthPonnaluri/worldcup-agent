[Are DSLR/professional cameras allowed?]
No. DSLR cameras, mirrorless cameras, video cameras, action cameras (like GoPros), selfie sticks, and professional recording equipment are strictly prohibited. Standard smartphones are allowed for personal photography and videography.

[Are power banks allowed inside the stadium?]
Yes, personal power banks are allowed inside the stadium to charge your mobile devices, but they must undergo security inspection at the entrance gates.

[Are personal water bottles or flasks allowed?]
No. Under BCCI and local security guidelines, all types of water bottles, thermos flasks, metal flasks, cans, and glass containers are strictly prohibited at all gates. Free drinking water is provided in paper cups at multiple water booths along the concourses of every stand.

[Can I bring outside food?]
No. Outside food, snacks, fruits, and drinks are not allowed inside the stadium perimeter. Stadium concessions, food stalls, and vendors inside serve regional snacks, meals, and beverages.

[Do I need a physical ticket to enter, or is an e-ticket/SMS confirmation enough?]
For almost all cricket matches in India (IPL, International bilaterals, ICC tournaments), e-tickets must be redeemed for physical tickets with a barcode/hologram before the match day. You cannot enter with an e-ticket PDF, email, or booking SMS. Ticket redemption counters are set up at designated partner locations or the stadium box office 2-3 days before the match.

[Is re-entry permitted if I leave the stadium?]
No. Once you exit through any gate, re-entry is strictly prohibited. Your ticket barcode will be invalidated upon first scan at the gate.

[What time do the gates open before a match?]
Gates typically open 3 hours prior to the scheduled kickoff time (e.g., gates open at 4:30 PM for a 7:30 PM match, and 11:00 AM for a 2:00 PM day-night match). Fans are strongly advised to arrive at least 2 hours early to clear the multi-tiered security checks.

[What is the ticketing policy for children?]
Children above 2 years of age require a full, individual ticket to enter. Children under 2 years do not require a ticket but must sit on their parent's or guardian's lap.

[Are umbrellas allowed if it rains?]
Only small, collapsible plastic umbrellas without metal tips are permitted. Long umbrellas, golf umbrellas, or those with metal points are strictly prohibited as they are deemed security hazards.

[Is smoking allowed inside the stadium?]
No. All five stadiums (Wankhede, Narendra Modi, Chinnaswamy, Rajiv Gandhi, Arun Jaitley) are strictly designated as non-smoking zones. E-cigarettes, vapes, matchboxes, and lighters are prohibited and will be confiscated at the gates.
