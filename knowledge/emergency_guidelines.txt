[First Aid and Medical Clinics]
For immediate medical help, visit the First Aid Station behind the North Stand (near Gate A) or the Apex Cardiac Care Unit located in Section 108. Stadium marshals can coordinate medical transit if required.

[Evacuation Procedure]
In the event of an emergency evacuation, stay calm and walk to the nearest designated exit gate. Do not run. Follow instructions broadcasted over the public address system.
