[Wankhede Stadium Mumbai Emergency & Medical]
- First Aid Clinics: Stationed behind the Garware Pavilion (Ground Floor) and near the North Stand Level 1.
- Advanced Cardiac Care: Fully equipped unit in Section 108 near Gate 3, managed in coordination with Bombay Hospital.
- Emergency Security Desk: Located at the Wankhede Pavilion Control Room. Contact: +91 22 2281 1123.
- Nearest Emergency Hospital: GT Hospital (Dhobi Talao) or St. George's Hospital (CSMT) - 10 minutes away.

[Narendra Modi Stadium Ahmedabad Emergency & Medical]
- First Aid Clinics: Available at every stand level. The main stadium medical center is located in the Club Pavilion (East Wing, Ground Floor).
- Cardiac Ambulances: Stationed behind Gate 1, Gate 3, and near the VIP Entrance. Apex Cardiac Unit is inside Section 112.
- Emergency Helpline: Stadium Security Room: +91 79 2750 2000.
- Nearest Emergency Hospital: KD Hospital (SG Highway) or Civil Hospital (Asarwa).

[M Chinnaswamy Stadium Bengaluru Emergency & Medical]
- First Aid Clinics: Located behind the P1 Stand (near Gate 2) and inside the KSCA Clubhouse (East Stand).
- Emergency Medical Booths: Staffed by HBS Hospital clinicians. A cardiac critical unit is set up near Section 10.
- Emergency Contact: Bengaluru Police Stadium Control Room: +91 80 2221 1789.
- Nearest Emergency Hospital: Bowring Hospital (Shivajinagar) or St. Martha's Hospital (Nrupathunga Road) - 5 minutes away.

[Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium Hyderabad Emergency & Medical]
- First Aid Clinics: Located near Gate 1 (South Pavilion) and Gate 8 (North Stand).
- Cardiac unit: Managed by Apollo Hospitals and stationed in Section 202. Ambulances are placed at the East and West ramp exits.
- Emergency Contact: Uppal Police Station Control Room: +91 40 2785 2400.
- Nearest Emergency Hospital: Tx Hospitals (Uppal) or Yashoda Hospitals (Secunderabad).

[Arun Jaitley Stadium Delhi Emergency & Medical]
- First Aid Clinics: Positioned inside the Old Club House (First Floor) and near the East Stand Gate 5.
- Cardiac Clinic: Located in Section 14 (Ground Floor) with support from Max Healthcare.
- Emergency Contact: Delhi Police Stadium Control Room: +91 11 2331 9991.
- Nearest Emergency Hospital: LNJP Hospital (Jawaharlal Nehru Marg) or GB Pant Hospital - directly adjacent to the stadium (1.5 km away).

[Evacuation & Safety Protocol - All Stadiums]
In case of fire, structural alert, or medical evacuation, stay calm and walk towards the nearest exit gate marked with green emergency signage. Do not run. Do not use lifts/elevators. Stadium announcements will play over the PA system with instructions. All exit routes are equipped with wide ramps to handle rapid crowd movement. Follow directions from stadium stewards and police personnel.
