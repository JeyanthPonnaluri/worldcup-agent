[Wankhede Stadium Mumbai Parking]
Wankhede Stadium does not have public parking on match days due to heavy congestion in the Churchgate/Marine Drive area. Roads surrounding the stadium (D Road, E Road, F Road) are closed to private vehicles except those with VIP passes. The nearest public parking is at Nariman Point (near Vidhan Bhavan or CR2 Mall), located 1.5 km (a 20-minute walk) away. Fans are strongly urged to use local trains to Churchgate Station.

[Narendra Modi Stadium Ahmedabad Parking]
Narendra Modi Stadium has 15 designated parking zones (Zones A to O) with space for over 10,000 cars and 20,000 two-wheelers. VIP Parking (Zones A-D) is adjacent to the main gates and requires a special BCCI permit. Public Parking (Zones E to O) is located around the Motera and Sabarmati areas. Pre-booking a parking pass via the ticketing partner (BookMyShow/Paytm Insider) is mandatory. The closest public lot is Zone G, which is 600 meters from Gate 1. Park-and-Ride shuttle service is available from the Janmarg BRTS stands.

[M Chinnaswamy Stadium Bengaluru Parking]
No public parking is available inside the M Chinnaswamy Stadium. Vehicles parked on surrounding roads (MG Road, Cubbon Road, Kasturba Road, Queens Road) will be immediately towed. Limited public parking is available at the UB City Mall parking lot (1 km away) or the Kanteerava Stadium parking lot (1.2 km away). Taking the Namma Metro to Cubbon Park Station is highly recommended.

[Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium Hyderabad Parking]
Public parking at Rajiv Gandhi Stadium is divided into designated lots outside the stadium perimeter. Cars are parked at the Genpact lane parking grounds or the Uppal Metro Station parking complex (800 meters from the stadium). Two-wheeler parking is available at the Exhibition Ground near Uppal crossroads. Security barricades prevent vehicles from approaching within 500 meters of the gates, so expect to walk.

[Arun Jaitley Stadium Delhi Parking]
There is no general parking inside the Arun Jaitley Stadium. VIP parking passes are required for the limited slots inside Kotla grounds. Public parking is available at the Mata Sundari College parking lot (1.2 km away), the Shanti Van parking ground (1.8 km away), or the Delhi Metro parking at Kashmere Gate (with shuttle transit). Feeder e-rickshaws run between these lots and the stadium gates.
