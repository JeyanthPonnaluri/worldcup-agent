[Wankhede Stadium Parking]
Wankhede Stadium Mumbai does not offer public parking on match days due to municipal congestion rules. Fans are strongly advised to use Western Railway local trains (Churchgate Station) or take taxis. If driving, the nearest public parking lot is at Nariman Point, which is approximately 1.5 km away from the gates.

[Narendra Modi Stadium Parking]
Narendra Modi Stadium Ahmedabad has 15 designated parking zones. Zones A-D are reserved for VIPs. Public parking is available at Zones E-O, requiring pre-purchased parking passes. It is highly recommended to take the Ahmedabad Metro to Sabarmati station to avoid parking queues.

[SoFi Stadium Parking]
SoFi Stadium parking lots open 4 hours before kickoff. Parking zones are color-coded: Blue, Green, Brown. Passes must be purchased in advance. Rideshare drop-off is at Lot N.
